The Best Movies According to Emily

My top 3 movies of all time.

Sound of Music

It's just a classic. You need to see it at least once. Or in my case, 100 times. A musical, history, romance, comedy. It's just all around great, especially for the time it was made in.

Cast Away

Basically a one man show. This movie will make you love Tom Hanks if you don't already. You are drawn into the plot and cast take your eyes off until the credits.

Cinderella-Disney Live Action

No fluff, just a good movie. Re-Watched many times.
